About Dave’s Donuts

Dave’s Donuts was founded on a simple idea: make people smile, one donut at a time. What started as a late-night craving turned into a full-blown passion for crafting the fluffiest, most flavorful donuts around. We believe that joy should come in dozens — glazed, filled, frosted, and everything in between.

Every donut we serve is made with love (and a bit of whimsy). From our first glaze to our most adventurous toppings, our mission has stayed the same — to bake up warm, welcoming treats for our community.

“Donuts are happiness in a circle — and we’re just here to share it.”

Whether you’re grabbing a quick dozen or just stopping by for your favorite frosted classic, Dave’s Donuts is always baking something sweet just for you.

Mini FAQ

Absolutely. Contact us 24 hours in advance for custom dozen or event boxes.

Do you make vegan donuts?

We offer vegan-friendly options every Friday and Saturday — check our menu or social for updates.
